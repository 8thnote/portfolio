A Little About Me

I've been developing websites for over ten years now, but it's only been within the last one to two that I've developed an intense desire to become an expert in the field (I even read the W3C specs). Before that I considered myself more of a multimedia generalist doing a lot of graphic design and video production in addition to web. I have been (and still am) privileged to call Central Technology Center my home away from home these last seven years; that's where I gained most of my experience leading teams on a wide range of projects (largely websites). But then I met Sass, Gulp and Angular, and my love for development sky rocketed!

Outside of work I have a beautiful wife and two cute, awnry kids. When I'm not working on web projects or spending time with my family, I like to go water skiing in the summer and snowboarding in the winter. I'm also a musician and an OKC Thunder fan. You can't beat Durant and Westbrook!

Latest Projects

Here are some samples of my latest work.

Mike Yeager - Pro Angler

Mike Yeager, a pro fisherman living in Tulsa, came to me in need of a new website to help him attract some new sponsors for his upcoming fishing season. We wanted to create a site that would make a big impression, but in a clear and straightforward way. I worked with him to design and develop a custom responsive site that he can easily make changes to directly in his browser.

See the Site

The Ministry Center

The Ministry Center provides impoverished Tulsa residents with free food and clothing. I was honored to be able to help their ministry with a fresh redesign of their website. It's a custom responsive site with a user friendly admin for them to make basic changes. The design is a big improvement over their previous site and now has a look and feel that they believe accurately reflects the heart of their ministry.
